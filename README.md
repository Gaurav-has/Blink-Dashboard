The goal was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive, single-page Power BI solution for real-time KPI monitoring, outlet performance comparison, and strategic decision support.

#Key Dashboard Insights

Financial Tracking: Monitored high-level metrics like $1.20M Total Sales and Avg Sales.

Operational Segmentation: Comparative analysis of sales volume and rating across different Outlet Location Tiers (Tier 1, 2, 3) and Outlet Size.

Product Strategy: Visualization of sales distribution by Item Type and FAT CONTENT (comparing Low Fat vs. Regular sales performance) to inform inventory stocking.

Time Series Analysis: Tracked the trend of Outlet Establishment over a multi-year period (2012-2022).

Interactivity: Utilized Slicers for dynamic filtering on Location, Size, and Item Type.
